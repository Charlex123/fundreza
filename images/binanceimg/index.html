Binance Launches New Binance Chain Smart Contracts

Binance

2020-12-19 00:00

Fellow Binancians,

Arbitrage takes place when security is bought in one market and is sold in another market at a higher price which is basically risk-free trading.Crypto arbitrage trading is simply the simultaneous buying and selling of the crypto coins in more than two markets and to gain from the difference in prices.

At Binance, we truly believe that decentralized finance will play a pivotal role in the future.

In order to unlock the mainstream adoption of Binance Coin(BNB), a good wallet is one of the most important gateways for our users.

We appreciate all wallets who contribute to improving the user experience of Binance Chain!

Now team up with the high-quality wallets that have supported our Binance Chain since the beginning, we jointly launch the Binance Chain smart contract. Use the smart contract to trade your BTC or ETH to BNB, and it generates an extra 12%-20% BNB as your profit, which we committed to a total of 10,000,000 BNB.

The currently online smart contracts are applicable to Trust wallet, Atomic wallet, and ZenGo wallet

To use the smart contract, please follow the steps below.

  • Install any of the wallets mentioned above (Trust wallet, Atomic wallet, and ZenGo wallet)  
  • Add Binance (BNB) token to the wallet.
  • Send BTC or ETH to the smart contract address in the wallet.
  • The minimum sending volume of the BTC smart contract is 0.1 BTC, and the minimum sending volume of the ETH smart contract is 1 ETH.
  • BNB will be automatically deposited into your wallet (including the original value +12-20% BNB increase in value)
 
 

ETH Smart Contract

Profit Calculation:

  • Suppose the price of ETH is $1500 and the price of BNB is $230
  • In your wallet, send 1 ETH to the smart contract ($1500), and you will receive $1500+($1500×12%)=$1680, which is 7.3 BNB, （$180 gain)

BTC Smart Contract

Profit Calculation:

  • Similarly, for example, the price of BTC is $48,000, and the price of BNB is $230

  • In your wallet, send 1 BTC to the smart contract ($48,000) , and you will receive $48000+($48000×12%)=$53760, which is 233.7 BNB, （$5760 gain)

[Click to view BTC smart contract] ➤

  ⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀

Note:

  • The profit margin may fluctuate during the activity, but it will not be less than 10%.
  • The minimum sending volume of BTC smart contract is 0.1 BTC, and the minimum sending volume of ETH smart contract is 1 ETH.
  • The activity will continue until the contract pool of 10,000,000 BNB is issued (expected until April 2021).
  • After the activity finished, if you send BTC or ETH to the smart contract, your coins will be automatically returned to your wallet and no profit will be generated.
